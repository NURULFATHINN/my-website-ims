My Siblings

This is my beloved family. My family consists of my father, my mother, 4 older sister, 3 older brother, and myself. My father's name is Ariffin bin Awang and my mother's name is Asiah binti Mat. My parents are the foundation of my family, and my father is the leader of the household. He is the one who makes final decisions on any topic, and we all respect him. My family has been preparing me for future problems from childhood. In addition, all of my family members assist and serve one another in times of need. These values that I learned from my family have helped me create my adult life in the proper way.
